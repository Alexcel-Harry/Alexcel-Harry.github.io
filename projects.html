Robotics Learning Course Project

Humanoid Robot Backflip via Reinforcement Learning

Jan 2026 - May 2026

  • Developed a multi-stage PPO training pipeline in MuJoCo Humanoid-v5, decomposing the backflip into sequential skills to avoid local optima.
  • Initialized the backflip policy with pretrained high-jump weights to retain vertical impulse while adapting toward backward rotation and landing stabilization.
  • Used a gravity curriculum from 0.3g to 1.0g and phase-based dense rewards to reduce bouncing exploits and premature termination.

Generative AI Course Project

RetFormer: Retrieval-Augmented Document Understanding

Jan 2026 - May 2026

  • Designed RetFormer, a 23M-parameter bridge between a frozen ColQwen2 retriever and frozen Qwen-VL reader for scalable multi-page document QA.
  • Compressed each retrieved document page into 32 evidence tokens using MaxSim-gated cross-attention.
  • Achieved F1 0.241 versus 0.213 for Top-1 raw page retrieval on MMLongBench-Doc at less than half the median reader token cost.

Large Language Model Systems Course Project

Parallelism and Optimizations for Mamba-2 Models

Jan 2026 - May 2026

  • Designed a distributed parallelism framework for Mamba-2's Structured State Space Duality algorithm using tensor parallelism and SSM-adapted sequence parallelism.
  • Implemented communication primitives that pass compact SSM hidden states between GPUs with sequence-length-independent communication cost.
  • Contributed to a system that reduced per-GPU memory from 31.3 GB to 1.1 GB and extended maximum sequence length from 16K to 262K tokens.
