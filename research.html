Research Experience

Professional research summaries.

Oct 2025 - Present
Carnegie Mellon University

End-to-End Adaptive Smart Glasses for Caregiving

Privacy-first wearable AI research focused on adaptive assistance under real-world edge constraints.

  • Developing an edge Vision-Language Model system with Multi-Timescale Meta-Continual Learning for adaptive sensor fusion across chronic monitoring and acute injury modes.
  • Implementing a Dual Working Memory system with Reinforcement Learning to personalize intervention timing while balancing utility and user disruption.
  • Designing an edge-only architecture to preserve privacy while staying responsive in time-sensitive caregiving scenarios.

May 2025 - Sept 2025
Open-source research project

Part-Level Scene Understanding with LLMs

Fine-grained part-level perception for robotic manipulation, accepted at IDETC/CIE 2026.

  • Constructed a Part-Segmentation-Relation dataset using Semantic-SAM and Gemini 2.5 for kinematic annotation of part functionalities and relationships.
  • Designed KAF-Net to predict part relationships using Kinematic Affinity Fields and Multi-Channel Masks.
  • Integrated KAF-Net with LLM planning to generate executable part-aware action sequences.

Mar 2024 - Apr 2025
UMich MCITY Lab

TeraSim: Generative Simulation for Autonomous Vehicle Safety

Generative traffic simulation work accepted at the SAD Workshop at CVPR 2026.

  • Engineered a SUMO-CARLA co-simulation plugin to inject dynamic roadblocks, vulnerable road users, and agent misfeasance into AV testing loops.
  • Converted and refined OpenDrive maps for high-fidelity MCITY test environment simulation.
  • Contributed to research that serves as the technical foundation for SaferDrive.ai.

Mar 2022 - Mar 2023
Shanghai Jiao Tong University

Magnetic Wristband Gesture Recognition

Undergraduate research on robust full-finger gesture recognition using magnetic sensing.

  • Optimized hardware configuration and signal-processing pipelines to achieve greater than 80% classification accuracy with high noise resistance.
  • Developed filtering and Euclidean-space geometric center analysis methods to isolate gesture signals across users and environments.
