Brief introduction

Machine learning depth with systems instincts.

I am a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University with strong foundations in machine learning and systems. My work spans learning-based models, AI infrastructure, simulation, edge deployment, and robotics-oriented perception and control.

I like problems where model behavior, systems constraints, and evaluation all matter at the same time: making models useful on-device, building simulations that reveal failures, or training agents that learn something interesting even when the first result is not the intended one.
